# Key City Digital — High-Converting Service Page Template (Texas Contractors)

## Purpose of This Template
This template is the reusable page framework for:
- Website Design for Contractors
- Local SEO for Contractors
- Google Maps Optimization for Contractors
- Facebook Ads for Contractors
- AI Chatbots for Contractors
- Branding & Logo Design for Contractors
- Video Content Creation for Contractors

It is designed for Texas home service owners who want more calls, more qualified leads, and more booked jobs.

**Conversion intent split:**
- Mid-funnel: push diagnostic action at `WebsiteLeakDetector.com`
- High-intent: push direct booking action at  
  `https://api.leadconnectorhq.com/widget/bookings/digital-marketing-consultation-apykl`

## 1) Hero Section

### Section Goal
State exactly who the service is for, what outcome it drives, and what to do next.

### Copy Formula
- **Headline:** `{Service Name} for Texas Contractors: {Primary Outcome}`
- **Subheadline:** `We help {audience} use {service} to generate {calls/leads/booked jobs outcome}.`
- **Proof line:** `Built for roofers, HVAC, plumbing, remodeling, electrical, landscaping, and more.`

### CTA Placement Guidance
- Primary button (mid-funnel): **Run Free Website Leak Detector**
- Secondary button (high-intent): **Book Consultation**
- Optional microcopy under buttons: “Not sure where to start? Run the free scan first.”

### UX Guidance
- Two-column hero: copy left, visual/proof panel right
- Keep paragraph lengths short (1–2 lines)
- Put CTAs above fold on desktop + mobile

### Trust Signal Recommendations
- Add quick trust bar beneath hero:
  - “Texas Contractor Focused”
  - “AI-Powered Execution”
  - “Conversion-First Strategy”

## 2) Why This Service Matters

### Section Goal
Create strategic context and urgency for this service specifically.

### Copy Formula
- **Lead sentence:** “In Texas markets, contractors lose jobs when {service gap} exists.”
- **Impact sentence:** “That gap leads to missed calls, lower trust, and fewer booked jobs.”
- **Opportunity sentence:** “When {service} is done right, lead flow and conversion improve fast.”

### CTA Placement Guidance
- Text CTA at end of section:
  - Mid-funnel pages (SEO/Maps/Content): Leak Detector CTA first
  - High-intent pages (Ads/AI/Branding): Book CTA first

### Internal Link Guidance
- Link to `/portfolio-results/` with anchor: “see how contractors improved results”

### UX Guidance
- Use 3 short benefit blocks (visibility, trust, conversion)
- Avoid long wall-of-text paragraphs

---

## 3) Problems We Solve

### Section Goal
Show deep understanding of contractor pain points and qualify the right buyer.

### Copy Formula
Use a **Problem → Cost → Fix** pattern for 4–6 bullets.

Example structure:
1. **Problem:** “Traffic comes in but leads stay low.”
   - **Cost:** “You pay for attention and get little revenue back.”
   - **Fix:** “We rebuild conversion paths and CTA clarity.”

2. **Problem:** “Leads are low quality or inconsistent.”
   - **Cost:** “Team time gets wasted on bad opportunities.”
   - **Fix:** “We tighten targeting, messaging, and qualification flow.”

### CTA Placement Guidance
- Place a compact CTA row under problem list:
  - “Run Free Leak Detector”
  - “Talk to a Strategist”

### Trust Signal Recommendations
- Add 1 mini case snippet in this section (“After fixing X, lead-to-call rate improved Y%”).

---

## 4) What’s Included

### Section Goal
Make the offer tangible and premium.

### Copy Formula
Use 6–8 included deliverables with clear outcome language:
- “{Deliverable} so you can {business outcome}.”

Example format:
- Strategy + audit
- Build/implementation
- Optimization cadence
- Reporting tied to calls/leads/bookings

### CTA Placement Guidance
- End section with internal link CTA to contact/booking:
  - “See how this is customized for your market” → `/contact/`

### UX Guidance
- Use icon cards or checklist cards
- Group items as: Strategy, Build, Optimize

### SEO Guidance
- Include semantic variants of service keyword naturally in deliverable names

## 8) FAQ Section

### Section Goal
Handle objections and move evaluation-stage users forward.

### Recommended FAQ Categories
1. Fit (“Is this for my trade?”)
2. Timing (“How fast can we launch?”)
3. Investment (“How do pricing and scope work?”)
4. Results (“When should we expect lead improvements?”)
5. Process (“What happens after the call?”)

### Copy Formula
- Keep answers short, direct, and outcome-focused.
- End final FAQ item with CTA transition sentence.

### CTA Placement Guidance
- Bottom of FAQ: **Book Consultation** button
- Secondary text link: Leak Detector

### UX Guidance
- Accordion format, 5–8 max questions
- Keep default closed except first item

### SEO Guidance
- FAQ can support rich results when structured data is implemented in production

## CTA Hierarchy by Intent

### Mid-Funnel Heavy Services
(Local SEO, GBP, Content)
- Hero: Leak Detector primary
- Mid-page: Leak Detector repeat
- Bottom: Booking primary

### High-Intent Services
(Facebook Ads, AI Chatbots, Branding)
- Hero: Booking and Leak Detector side-by-side
- Mid-page: Booking slightly prioritized
- Bottom: Booking primary, Leak Detector secondary

### Universal Rule
Every service page should present **both** conversion paths, but sequence them based on likely intent.

## UX + Design Notes for Premium Feel

- Dark premium sections with gold + electric blue accents
- Bold typography with large headline contrast
- Plenty of spacing and clean card hierarchy
- Short, direct copy blocks (no fluff)
- Sticky top CTA (Leak Detector) and optional floating mobile booking CTA
- Repeat CTA bands every 2–3 sections

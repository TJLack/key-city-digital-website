# Content Distribution Plan (Traffic Amplification)

## Repurposing Workflow per Resource Article
1. **Short-form video (30-60s):** one tactical lesson + CTA to article.
2. **Social post thread:** key framework + city/industry context.
3. **Email insight:** weekly digest linking to newest resource + service CTA.

## Channel Plan
- LinkedIn: strategy breakdowns + market insights
- Instagram/Facebook: short clips + carousel tips for contractors
- YouTube Shorts: FAQ answers with city/industry framing
- Email newsletter: weekly "Texas Contractor Growth Brief"

## CTA Routing
- Informational traffic -> `https://WebsiteLeakDetector.com`
- High-intent traffic -> booking widget

## Weekly Cadence
- Publish 2 resource articles
- Publish 4 short videos
- Publish 6-8 social posts
- Send 1 email digest

## KPIs
- Session growth to `/resources/`
- Assisted conversions from resource pages
- CTA click-through rates (scan + booking)
- Indexed pages and time-to-index by tier

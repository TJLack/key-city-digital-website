# Safe Backlink Strategy (Texas Contractor Authority)

## Goal
Build authority with trusted, relevant mentions that reinforce "digital marketing for Texas contractors" without spam patterns.

## Priority Sources (Quality First)
1. Texas/local business directories (city chambers, regional associations, local startup directories)
2. Contractor ecosystem sites (software partners, suppliers, financing vendors)
3. Partner/client websites (case-study citations, partner pages)
4. Industry podcasts/newsletters (expert commentary + resource links)
5. Select guest contributions on reputable contractor blogs

## Outreach Framework
- Publish a resource article + local proof point.
- Pitch one specific insight (not generic agency promotion).
- Request contextual links to resource hub and relevant service page.
- Track acceptance rate and referring-domain quality.

## Guardrails
- Avoid low-quality link farms, paid bulk links, and private blog networks.
- Avoid exact-match anchor overuse; maintain natural anchor diversity.
- Cap new referring domains to controlled growth (e.g., 6-12/month initially).

## Anchor Text Mix
- 45% branded: "Key City Digital"
- 30% partial match: "Texas contractor marketing"
- 15% topical long-tail: "local SEO for HVAC contractors in Fort Worth"
- 10% generic: "learn more"

## Landing Targets
- Resource hub: `/resources/index.html`
- Pillar services: `/services/local-seo-contractors.html`, `/services/website-design-contractors.html`, `/services/google-business-profile-optimization.html`
- Tier 1 local pages from `pseo/sitemap-tier1.xml`

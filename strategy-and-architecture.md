## 2) Content Strategy (Lead + Booking System)

### Core conversion journey
1. **Awareness**: Texas contractor lands from search/social/referral.
2. **Diagnosis CTA**: Prominent link to `WebsiteLeakDetector.com`.
3. **Authority Build**: Service proof, trade-specific messaging, testimonials.
4. **High-intent CTA**: Book consultation via embedded booking calendar.
5. **Secondary conversion**: Contact form for questions.

### Messaging pillars
- Builders of Local Legends
- Texas-proud + trade-specific positioning
- AI-enabled execution with human strategy
- Lead quality + booking velocity, not vanity metrics

### Internal linking strategy
- Homepage links to every core service page.
- Every service page links back to service hub, contact, and lead magnet.
- Industries and Texas markets pages deep-link into relevant services.
- Footer repeats lead magnet + booking links globally.

## 4) Copy Architecture (before final copy)

### Voice and style system
- Direct-response language, short paragraphs, concrete outcomes.
- Avoid generic agency words and corporate fluff.
- Keep reading level simple while preserving premium positioning.

### Homepage copy blocks
- Headline formula: audience + pain + promised outcome.
- Subheadline formula: capabilities + differentiator + local relevance.
- CTA pair: “Run Leak Detector” + “Book Consultation”.

### Service copy blocks
- Problem diagnosis: why contractors lose leads today.
- Mechanism: what KCD installs and optimizes.
- Offer stack: deliverables + timeline + expected impact.
- Conversion CTA repeated at top/mid/bottom.

### Trust/credibility blocks
- Trade-specific testimonials.
- Results snapshots.
- Texas-market understanding and local search expertise.

### SEO architecture
- One clear H1 per page.
- Logical H2/H3 hierarchy.
- Trade + location modifier language naturally included.
- FAQ schema-ready sections (semantic HTML structure prepared).

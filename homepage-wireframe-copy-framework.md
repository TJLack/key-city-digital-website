# Key City Digital — Conversion-Focused Homepage Wireframe + Copy Framework

## Strategic Objective
Build a homepage that feels premium, Texas-authoritative, and conversion-intentional for home service contractors.

**Primary actions:**
1. Run free scan at `WebsiteLeakDetector.com` (cold + problem-aware traffic)
2. Book consultation via calendar (warm/high-intent traffic):  
   `https://api.leadconnectorhq.com/widget/bookings/digital-marketing-consultation-apykl`

---

## Conversion Logic (Homepage Level)
- **Above the fold:** Dual CTA with Leak Detector as primary, Book Consultation as secondary.
- **Mid-page:** Leak Detector repeated in diagnostic/problem sections.
- **Lower-page:** Consultation CTA emphasized after trust, proof, and process.
- **Sticky header CTA:** Leak Detector.
- **Sticky/floating mobile CTA (optional):** Book Consultation.

## Messaging Guardrails (Keep Copy Non-Generic)
- Speak directly to contractors and owners/operators.
- Tie every claim to an outcome (calls, leads, booked jobs, close-rate quality).
- Avoid empty agency language unless supported by specifics.
- Maintain confident Texas tone: direct, premium, no fluff.
